## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Royal Gold | `#D4AF37` | Primary — Logo, accents, CTAs |
| Deep Maroon | `#800020` | Secondary — Headers, borders |
| Ivory Cream | `#FFF8E7` | Background — Website, cards |
| Charcoal Black | `#1A1A1A` | Text — Body copy |
| Rose Gold | `#B76E79` | Accent — Highlights, badges |
| Deep Teal | `#005050` | Contrast — Rare accent pops |

### Typography

| Style | Font | Usage |
|-------|------|-------|
| Display/Headings | **Cinzel** (Google Fonts) | Logo, H1-H3 — regal, serif elegance |
| Body Text | **Playfair Display** (Google Fonts) | Paragraphs — sophisticated readability |
| Accents | **Cormorant Garamond** (Google Fonts) | Quotes, testimonials — poetic flair |
| UI/Labels | **Lato** (Google Fonts) | Buttons, nav, small text — clean contrast |

### Logo Variations
1. **Primary:** Detailed zari-embroidered 'R' with golden threadwork
2. **Minimal:** Clean 'R Company' in Cinzel, gold on maroon
3. **Icon-only:** The embroidered 'R' standalone (for favicon, social avatar)
4. **Horizontal:** Full name with tagline, for headers
5. **Vertical:** Stacked version, for square formats (Instagram)

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Personality Matrix

| Dimension | Position |
|-----------|----------|
| Formal ↔ Casual | **Formal-leaning** (premium but warm) |
| Traditional ↔ Modern | **Bridging both** — traditional craft, modern presentation |
| Loud ↔ Subtle | **Subtle confidence** — quality speaks |
| Young ↔ Mature | **Sophisticated** — bridal, timeless |
| Emotional ↔ Practical | **Emotional** — stories, heritage, love |

### Voice Guidelines

```
DO:                              DON'T:
- "Handcrafted with heritage"    - "BEST ZARI EVER!!!"
- "Where tradition meets you"    - "Cheap rates, call now"
- "Every thread has a story"     - "Viral zari sale 🔥"
- Speak like a master artisan    - Speak like a street vendor
- Warm, inviting, luxurious      - Cold, corporate, generic
```

### Content Pillars

1. **The Craft** — Behind-the-scenes of zari embroidery process
2. **The Stories** — Customer stories, bridal moments, tradition
3. **The Style** — How to style, fashion tips, trend insights
4. **The Heritage** — History of zari, cultural significance
5. **The Collection** — Product showcases, launches, previews
